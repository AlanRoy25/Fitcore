WHAT ACTUALLY WORKS?
✅ Creatine — Most proven. +5-10% strength. 5g/day. Always.
✅ Whey Protein — Convenient protein. Use if not hitting food protein goal.
✅ Vitamin D3 — Most Indians are deficient. 2000-5000 IU/day.
✅ Omega-3 — Anti-inflammatory, heart + joint health.
✅ Magnesium — Better sleep, muscle recovery, reduces stress.
⚠️ Pre-workout — Helps energy but causes dependency. Use 3x/week max.
⚠️ BCAA — Unnecessary if eating enough protein. Save money.
❌ Fat burners — Don't waste money. Diet + cardio does this.
